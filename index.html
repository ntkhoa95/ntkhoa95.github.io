Research

My research interests mainly include timeseries data, visual perception, and image segmentation for autonomous driving. Specifically, I am working on various techniques from traditional image processing to taking the advantages of deep learning methods to develop efficient self-supervised approachs for segmentation using RGB-D data as inputs.
