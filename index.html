Research

My research interests mainly include visual perception and visual navigation for autonomous driving. Specifically, I am working on the computation of visual features including stereo matching, optical flow estimation and surface normal estimation. I am also focusing on improving the perception and navigation performance by exploiting the aforementioned visual features. Representative works are highlighted (* indicates equal contribution).
